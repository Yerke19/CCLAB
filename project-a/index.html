Family:

Hypnosium Star's ancestars are ancient starfishes on Earth. It's an advacned form of them, with the change of the habit and ability to develop multiple modifications durings its short lifetime.

Habitat:

This creature migrated to a new habitat - an air or sky, which is different from its ancestors that lived in water or earth. Because of that, the name of it is no longer a starfish, but a star as it is a medium stage between starfish and a star that is in the cosmos.

Appearance:

The Everolving Star's main body is represented by the simpler form of the star that can change its shape, increasing sharpness in the second half of its lifetime. The modifications that appear inside of the star are additionally developed bodies. Each of these bodies have their own shapes, either bubbly, sharp or blossoming, replacing one another as the star’s lifetime goes on. They have distinguished pink-ish and blue-ish colors, signifying their relevance to the main body.

Key Characteristics:

  • Has limited lifetime'
  • Experiences 4 main stages: development, progress, wilt and final blossom represented by the changes in main and additional bodies' features
  • Can move throughout the environment and leave traces of its shape.

What we know so far about Everolving Star? Everolving Star is an evolutionary organism with its genetic background going back to the ancient starfishes on Earth. It has developed to the point where starfishes were able to ascend and make their appearance visible to anyone who looks up as it has not yet gone far into the cosmos. However, the lifetime of each star is extremely limited, happening most likely during the night time according to the surrounding darkness. The momentum to catch the stages of Everolving Star is less than 2 minutes. The name “Everolving” comes from two words “ever” and “evolving” due to the star’s continuous changes of shape during four remarkable stages, never stopping, forever losing previous features and adapting new modifications with every new appearance.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creatureThe creature ascends and starts its development shortly after. You may interact with it by calling it to come to different places within its environment, but you cannot prevent it from the evolutionary process that will happen regardless of its position. Enjoy the beauty of it while it lasts!
