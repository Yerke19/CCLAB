Hypnosium Star

Scientific Name:

Hypnosium Star

Discovered by / at:

Dr. Degelenovna at 04:29am in 2???

Family:

Hypnosium Star's ancestars are ancient starfishes on Earth. It's an advacned form of them, with the change of the habit and certain features

Habitat:

This creature migrated to a new habitat - an air or sky, which is different from its ancestors that lived in water or earth. Because of that, the name of it is no longer a starfish, but a star as it is a medium stage between starfish and a star in the cosmos.

Appearance:

It has a unique shape of a star that may appear with different colors and a spinning arrow inside of it.

Key Characteristics:

  • Divides into multiple similar organisms
  • Develops new colors;
  • Has a “hand of time” inside of it

What we know so far about Hypnosium Star? Hypnosium Star is an evolutionary organism with its genetic background going back to the ancient starfishes on Earth. It has developed to the point where starfishes were able to “fly” to the sky and make their appearance visible to anyone who looks up. The creature can divide into many versions of it, each having its own color. By having an inner arrow that reminds the hand of time constantly spinning, the star hypnotizes the one that looks on it for too long, hence the name. It multiplies with the aim to protect itself from external intervention as it starts to divide because of it happening; It dies shortly after multiplying.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature The creature disappears from the sight of others shortly after being bothered, but it shows its beauty before fading away. Thus, it is up to each to decide whether they want to keep the star the way it is and make it last longer or reach to it to see a bright image but that will disappear shortly.
